Frequently Asked Questions About Solar Power in Lagos

Common questions homeowners and businesses ask before installing solar power systems.

How much does solar installation cost in Lagos? +

The cost depends on how much electricity your property needs and which appliances you want to power. After a quick assessment, we provide a system recommendation tailored to your budget and energy usage.

How long does solar installation take? +

Most residential solar installations are completed within one to three days depending on system size and roof complexity.

Can solar power run a refrigerator and other appliances? +

Solar systems can power common appliances such as refrigerators, televisions, lighting, fans and computers. During your assessment, HelioCore designs a system sized for the appliances you rely on every day.

How long do solar panels last? +

Most solar panels last 20-25 years. HelioCore installs high-quality systems designed for long-term performance and reliability.

Do I still need a generator with solar? +

Many customers significantly reduce generator use after installing solar. In some cases the generator becomes a backup used only occasionally.

Do solar panels work during rainy season? +

Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy or rainy days, although production may be slightly lower. Battery storage helps maintain consistent power during these periods.

How long do solar batteries last? +

Most modern solar batteries last between 5-10 years depending on usage. HelioCore installs high-quality systems designed for long-term performance.

Can I start small and upgrade my system later? +

Yes. Many customers begin with a system that powers essential appliances and expand over time as their needs grow.
